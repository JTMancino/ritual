Privacy Policy

This site is hosted on github pages, and user authentication as well as database management is run through Supabase. This site therefore inherits both of their privacy policies, as applicable. No information is collected about users outside of these functions. Your Supabase authentication account, associated with your email, is used to tie your identity to the characters you create so that you can access them later and to prevent others from accessing and altering characters you create without permission.


If you would like to delete your account or any data associated with it, you may contact me at my personal email john.t.mancino@gmail.com. If I do not respond within a reasonable or convenient period, or if you prefer not to contact me directly, please contact Supabase for support.

Artificial Intelligence Policy

Some of the code used in the creation of this website was created with generative artificial intelligence large language model (LLM) tools. All code produced with LLM assistance has been manually reviewed by humans to ensure safety and security. Some or all images displayed on this website are made with generative artificial intelligence tools, and while the site remains free this will continue to be the case. No paid content will ever directly feature artificial intelligence artwork.
